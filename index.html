About Us

Welcome to SunRise.It embraces minimalist lines that make seamless connections with the surroundings, thus skillfully encapsulating the island's enchanting dynamism. The swimming pool is a natural extension of the sea and the horizon in the distance, making SunRise HOTEL an unsurpassed hotel in Santorini with sunset view. Everything here is in perfect balance, ready to welcome guests into vibrant spaces that will inspire, showcasing the essence of Santorinian hospitality coupled with exceptional services for a stay beyond anticipation.

Best Hotels For You & Your Family

Hotel The Panache

The Panache provides comfortable 4-star accommodation in Patna. It is conveniently located for those wishing to sightsee in the area.

Hotel Maris

The hotel offers a pure vegetarian restaurant – Anandam – that serves simple and wholesome South Indian cuisine. The Permit Room offers a wide range of imported and local alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.
Two unique banquet halls are available at Maris. The spaces can be tailored to suit any type of function or meeting ranging from 30 guests to 200 guests

The Luminous Haven

The pool front sea view rooms feature a modern mix ‘n’ match approach with a king-size bed and a bathroom with a rain shower-jet. They are located on the ground floor with direct access to the hotel’s central swimming pool and offer the ultimate in noise insulation, comfort, and privacy while boasting exceptional sea & sunset views from the 22-29 m2 their outdoor patio.
